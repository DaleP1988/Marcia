Welcome to Milchiker.com!

This page contains information about Marcia Milchiker and the Milchiker family. Marcia is running for reelection in 2014 for her position as Trustee of the South Orange County Community College District, which contains Saddleback College and Irvine Valley College.

Marcia is a strong supporter of education at all levels. She has helped set up and maintain educational programs at Saddleback College and Irvine Valley College for all ages, from pre-school to Emeritus, and is especially committed to maintaining and strengthening the wonderful educational experience the South Orange County Community Colleges provide to their students.

Marcia appreciates your support, both in her campaign and in her mission to maintain and improve the South Orange County Community Colleges. Send her an email and get on the mailing list to find out what you can do to help our community colleges!
